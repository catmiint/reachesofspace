Reaches of Space

Welcome to the Reaches of Space, where we follow the lives and times of Gummisaurs — incredible shape shifting gummy dragons exploring the galaxy. Gummisaurs are an 18+ combined Humanoid/Feral Closed Species concepted by catmiint & owned by catmiint and aferael.

Traveling between stars and planets, Gummisaurs hope to experience new places, meet new people, and add to their hoards. Their highly resilient plasma gummy allows their draconic forms to resist the dangers of open space, while their adaptable humanoid forms allow them to walk amongst planets’ inhabitants.
