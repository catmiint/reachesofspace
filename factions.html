Ouranous Kynigi

Ouranous Kynigi (aka the Celestial Hunt, Leviathan Hunters) are a group known for their wits and martial prowess. Their primary target is Leviathans, the myth-like predators of gummisaurs, although they do sometimes take on other dangerous quarry across the reaches of space.
They have relatively open enrollment for fellow gummisaurs, although most official hunters have to go through a series of trials to both hone their skills and show how they can handle themselves in a variety of situations for their own safety as well as the safety of others. The job can be tricky, after all. As for other species, they don’t have many, if any, in their ranks - their advertising is generally more specific to the specific prey of Leviathans, regardless of the nature of their other jobs at the current moment. Perhaps, with time, this will change.
The Kynigi most often sends out its hunting parties in multiples of two, with an even amount of members from both standards preferred even in the most treacherous of emergency situations. This helps balance out the strengths and weaknesses both parties face and facilitates stronger, more effective results from hunts.

Apollo's Lyre

Apollo’s Lyre houses those whose fighting prowess may vary, but other useful skills go unmatched. Their group can tend to seem more mismatched, but fits under the gold and red of the Sun standard all the same.
Common skillsets include:
  • Healing and Medical Care
  • Documentation and Research
  • Ranged or Supportive fighting styles
This division manages the administrative paperwork and assignments, as well as most tasks that don’t fit into the other divisions niche. As such, this division tends to have slightly more people under it’s umbrella to both keep up with their own tasks and still have members to assist with the Hunt.

Artemis' Quiver

Artemis’ Quiver finds its strengths deep in its marital prowess first and most other aspects separate. The silver and blue Moon standard welcomes all who desire to fight for its cause tooth and nail, although they also have their fair share of other strengths too.
Common skillsets include:
  • Tracking and Navigation
  • Mentorship and Teaching
  • Knowledge of the Field and Quarry
This division is more heavily focused on the hunt, running on gut feelings and an unmatched knowledge of both fighting and their quarry only earned by being most commonly out on the hunting field. They are also tasked with the training and supervision of the Kynigi’s newest members.

Home Base

The Speira System

The Speira system is a notably interesting locale, spiralling through space in orbit around the remains of a dead star formerly referred to as Sahar. The orbit the whole system takes is growing looser as external gravities slowly pull it off the course it remains on, but there’s somehow some remainder of force from Sahar to keep it going in orbit - at least, for now.

The heart of the Speira System is a white dwarf star known as RX-013 on Earth, but more locally known by the name Astraura. The light it sheds is minimal due to its size, causing some of the planets to develop their own light in the form of bioluminescence. It gives off just enough heat to keep the three planets orbiting it habitable, which is honestly astounding for such a weak star.
